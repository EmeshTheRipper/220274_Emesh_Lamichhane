What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking, also known as "penetration testing," is the practice of testing a computer system, network, or web application to find security vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers. Ethical hackers use the same tools and techniques as malicious hackers, but with the goal of identifying and fixing vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by attackers.
